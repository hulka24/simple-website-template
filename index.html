Clinical psychologist

In my role as a psychologist, I want to adapt by treatment, so it best fits each client. I have experience working with transdiagnostic psychotherapies such as unified protocol, affect phobia therapy and relational therapy. In addition to that I have specific competence with using mental imagery in therapy.
